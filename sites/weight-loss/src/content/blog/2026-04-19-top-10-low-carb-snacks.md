# Top 10 Low Carb Snacks for Weight Loss

## Best Low Carb Snack Options

1. Hard-boiled eggs
2. Cheese crisps
3. Mixed nuts
4. Celery with almond butter
5. Greek yogurt
6. Pork rinds
7. Cucumber slices with cream cheese
8. Olives
9. Beef jerky (no sugar)
10. Avocado slices

## Why Low Carb Snacks Work

Low carb snacks help:
- Control hunger
- Maintain stable blood sugar
- Burn fat more efficiently
