# How to Start Keto Diet: 7 Day Meal Plan

## What is the Keto Diet?

The ketogenic diet is a low-carb, high-fat diet that puts your body into ketosis - a state where it burns fat for fuel instead of carbohydrates.

## 7 Day Keto Meal Plan

### Day 1
- Breakfast: Eggs and bacon
- Lunch: Chicken salad with olive oil
- Dinner: Salmon with asparagus

### Day 2
- Breakfast: Omelette with cheese
- Lunch: Tuna salad
- Dinner: Beef stir-fry with vegetables

## Tips for Success

1. Track your macros
2. Stay under 20g net carbs
3. Don't fear healthy fats
4. Prepare meals in advance
