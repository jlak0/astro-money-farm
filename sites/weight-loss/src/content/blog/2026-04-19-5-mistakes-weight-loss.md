# 5 Mistakes That Stop Your Weight Loss

## Mistake 1: Eating Too Many Calories
Even "healthy" foods can cause weight gain if eaten in excess.

## Mistake 2: Not Getting Enough Sleep
Poor sleep increases hunger hormones and slows metabolism.

## Mistake 3: Relying on Exercise Alone
You can't out-train a bad diet.

## Mistake 4: Being Too Restrictive
Extreme diets lead to binge eating.

## Mistake 5: Not Tracking Progress
What gets measured gets managed.

## Solution

Focus on sustainable habits, not quick fixes.
