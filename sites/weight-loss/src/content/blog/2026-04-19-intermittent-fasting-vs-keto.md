## Key Differences

**Intermittent Fasting** controls WHEN you eat
**Keto Diet** controls WHAT you eat

## Can You Combine Them?

Yes! In fact, many people find that combining IF and keto:
- Accelerates weight loss
- Improves mental clarity
- Increases energy levels

## Which Should You Choose?

- **Choose IF** if you want simpler rules
- **Choose Keto** if you want specific food guidelines
- **Combine both** for maximum results
