# 16:8 Intermittent Fasting - Complete Beginner's Guide

## What is 16:8 Intermittent Fasting?

The 16:8 method is one of the most popular forms of intermittent fasting. You fast for 16 hours and eat within an 8-hour window.

## How to Get Started

### Step 1: Choose Your Eating Window
Most people prefer eating from 12pm to 8pm, skipping breakfast.

### Step 2: Start Slowly
Begin with a 12-hour fast and gradually extend to 16 hours.

### Step 3: Stay Hydrated
Drink plenty of water, black coffee, or unsweetened tea during your fast.

## Benefits

- Weight loss
- Improved insulin sensitivity
- Simpler meal planning
- May increase longevity

## Tips for Success

1. Start on weekends when you're less busy
2. Keep meals simple at first
3. Don't overeat during your eating window
4. Listen to your body
